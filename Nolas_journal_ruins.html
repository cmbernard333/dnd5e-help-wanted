The writing in the journal is done in an unsteady hand. Glancing through the rest of the journal, you find the handwriting deteriorates following the third entry.

  • Entry 1

    • We set out across the desert today. The Zhentrim have promised us 1000 GP to deliver our goods as soon as possible. The desert is not a welcoming place for any traveler. It is mostly devoid of moisture and plant life. The creatures that live here are even less friendly. Let us hope we do not run into any of the raiding Bedine tribes.

  • Entry 2

    • We are half way across the desert. We are running low on water and Grimnar is starting to get aggressive with the rations. Orlon spotted a lonely pile of ruins when scanning atop the bluffs. We will most likely make shelter there before we finish our journey.

  • Entry 3

    • Cold. So Cold. The Anauroch desert isn't supposed to be cold. Something, something brought the cold. We saw a flash of light shoot up into the sky where the former Empire of Shadows once stood. We were not prepared for this. Orlon and Grimnar have been fighting even more now that our water is dangerously low.

  • Entry 4

    • This is it. We die here. Grimnar and Orlon's spat came to a head and they mortally wounded one another after we made it to the ruins. I lie here, too cold to move, and wrestle with the thought that I might need to eat one of them to survive. To those that find this: I Nola Delane write my last words. If you find me, please don't eat me. Bury me.
