My demoness,

The writing of this letter is in a script that you haven't read since you were very young. It is the script that you and your childhood friend Garon invented to send each other secret messages.

My demoness,

To had a hunch oe takin biak my ba labne evening.
[I had a hunch you would head my way yesterday evening.]

The same sixth sense also warned to oe kutin hibne forlin ti.
[The same sixth sense also warned me you might not make it.

To spotted oe bina oe walked into the chapel, moky decided sy hang sakam and tryn beti oe takin tun sakam baker.
[I spotted you as you walked into the chapel, but decided to hang back and see if you would come back out.]

Another rosati, seemingly ly a hurry, was ka his ba sy follow oe ly moky the bite ro drow mala gave ti a sweet goodnight.
[Another monk, seemingly in a hurry, was on his way to follow you in but the bite of drow poison gave him a sweet goodnight.]

To will be heryan sy oe and to'm aware ro laf the children ro bysalik are planning. Sarhon lit to.
[I will be close to you and I'm aware of what the Children of Winter are planning. Look for me.]
