Journal of Elminster Aumar

The writing started in a fine print, but as its progresses it transforms into a scribbled hand as if the words themselves were frantic.

  • Midsummer, a year ago

    • The memories of that day are returning. The cries of the hunters, the blood, the screams, the fire at the church. How long has it been? 50 years? So many taken. Althea, why did you bid me go?

  • Fall, a year ago

    • I hear her in my dreams now. She talks of the ties of blood. The ties of family. Her voice is sweet. She tells me The Blood is strong. Have no fear. I am old now. Well beyond my adventuring days. My bones are old. My blood is old.

  • Winter, a year ago

    • The nightmares are strong now. She is there and she says she is trapped. She needs my help. She promises strength. Youth. The Blood is strong. Have no fear.

  • Early Spring

    • I set off tomorrow with my old friend, the lovable oaf that he is, Moragog and his traveling companions. I know not what awaits me between Leilon and the Cathedral. I know only that She lies within. The Blood is strong. Have no fear.
