Early Alchemy Experiment

The writing is incredibly ancient and parts of the page have crumbled away. It appears to be a recipe but nothing in the writing indicates what for.

...Calamine and Opriment given and Cuprite binds them well. This time I will attempt Aqua Regia instead of Aqua Fortis in hope that it will produce a more even solution. Mix together and apply a flame...

...The experiment was unsuccessful. The solution is highly acidic and proves impractical to put to any use except as a detergent. Especially bad for organic material...
