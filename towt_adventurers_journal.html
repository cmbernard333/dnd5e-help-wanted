You flip through a couple pages and spot the first legible entry. It is scrawled in a hasty hand.

  • Entry 1
    • This is the log of the adventurer, in the prime of his life, Theodore Crumpet. I descended into the crater without incident I make camp here and make my way to the wizards tower tomorrow at dawn.

  • Entry 2

    • I managed to distract and escape the shambling rock beast today using my lucky amulet. It took hours. I was greeted by two dim witted gargoyles. I convinced them I was an emissary from the far away kingdom of Grand hall and they allowed me to pass. Making note here so I don't forget my story.

  • Entry 3

    • It became dark early this day so I went into the tower. I thought I had heard the cries of a man as I passed the threshold, but I think it was my imagination. I make camp inside the tower and tomorrow morning I will explore it.

  • Entry 4

    • This is my final entry. I awoke to find that piece of furniture, not sure which, had come to life and was gnawing on my leg. I write this as the beast continues to slowly consume me. Lucky my journal was nearby. To whomever reads this: run and get out as soon as you can!
