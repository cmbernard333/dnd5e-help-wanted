The Journal of Asha

The writing in the journal is written eloquently in almost a calligraphic way. The clasp seems to have been hastily closed as the lock was not secure.

  • Entry 1:

    • Jeremiah insists that I must marry him. He won't take no for an answer. He comes every day to appeal to my father for my hand in marriage. I may not love him, ever, but he is kind and I know he would make a good husband.

  • Entry 2:

    • I have gone against my father's wishes and against my family's name. I have lain with Maxine. She is who I love more above all else, though her tendency for anger has me worried.

  • Entry 3:

    • Jeremiah found out about my secret rendevous with Maxine on accident. We had been using the old panic room in my parents summer estate and he must have heard us. Rather than talk, Maxine pulled her blade almost instantly. I told Jeremiah if he'd tell nothing to my father I would wed him. After Jeremiah left, Maxine struck me and left.

  • Entry 4:

    • Jeremiah has convinced my father that I should wed him. Maxine is upset beyond all. She told me she would rather die than me marry Jeremiah.

    ..page here is torn and part of it is missing so the writing is incomplete...
    ...asked that we share it publically at the engagement party to show our love. Agreed without hesitation.

  • Entry 5:

    • I went to Jeremiah today as I have done for several other days since the engagement was announced. Today was special...

    ...the writing here appears to be smudged with water coming from above...
    ...the plan is simple. Tonight things will be resolved.
